Bitcoin at SXSW 2015

March 16th 2015, Austin, TX

about conference

Bitcoin at SXSW 2015 will take place on Monday, March 16th at SXSWi’s Startup Village, located in the Hilton Downtown Austin. The sessions will take place in the Austin Grand Ballroom (Salon G) on the 6th floor of the Hilton’s convention center.
Our goal is to educate, engage, and excite you about Bitcoin! We want you to spend the day with us and feel like an expert on the topic by the end of the day! We have a great lineup of speakers from big bitcoin companies to non-profits.
