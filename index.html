Bitcoin at SXSW 2015

March 16th 2015, Austin, TX

about conference

Bitcoin at SXSW 2015 will take place on Monday, March 16th at SXSWi’s Startup Village, located in the Hilton Downtown Austin. The sessions will take place in the Austin Grand Ballroom (Salon G) on the 6th floor of the Hilton’s convention center. Our goal is to excite you about Bitcoin!

We want you to spend the day with us and feel like an expert on the topic by the end of the day! We have a great lineup of speakers from big bitcoin companies to non-profits.

Schedule

Click on the titles for more information

9:30 - 10:30 AM

What is Bitcoin?

Presented by BitPay.com - A panel discussion covering the history of Bitcoin, what it is, & it’s implications as a global digital currency.

Sean Neville, Nic Cary, Stephen Pair, & Pamela Morgan
11:00 - 12:00 PM

Bitcoin 2.0

Presented by Gyft.com - A panel discussion on how to improve Bitcoin including the role of sidechains, altcoins & exchanges.

Vitalik Buterin, Joyce Kim, Charlie Lee, & Constance Choi
12:30 - 1:30 PM

A Future with Bitcoin

Presented by LibraTax.com - Four talks on topics that are relevant to Bitcoin becoming a part of mainstream culture and finance.

Marco Santori, Jake Benson, Dan Elitzer, & Andreas Antonopoulos
3:30 - 4:30 PM

Impact on Developing World

Presented by ChangeTip.com - A panel discussion covering how Bitcoin is empowering the developing world through banking, charity, and more.

Sebastian Serrano, Connie Gallippi, Peter Smith, & Sean Percival
5:00 - 6:00 PM

Real World Applications

Presented by Chain.com - A panel on why Bitcoin is a groundbreaking platform, what entrepreneurs are doing with it, & future technologies.

Vinny Lingham, Nick Sullivan, Adam Ludwin, & Adam Draper
